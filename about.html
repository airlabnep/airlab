About Agri-Intelligence Research Lab

Advancing geospatial technologies for sustainable agriculture in Nepal

Our Mission

Agri-Intelligence Research Lab is a spatial research laboratory dedicated to bridging the gap between cutting-edge geospatial technologies and practical agricultural applications. Based in Nepal, we focus on developing and implementing drone-based precision agriculture solutions, computer vision systems, and AI-driven decision support tools that empower farmers with data-driven insights.

We believe that by combining remote sensing, spatial analysis, and agricultural science, we can help create more sustainable, efficient, and climate-smart farming practices.

Our Vision

To become a leading center of excellence in spatial research and precision agriculture, transforming how farmers across Nepal and South Asia make decisions using geospatial data and artificial intelligence.

Core Values

♻️ Sustainability

Research Areas

Precision Nutrient Management

Using multispectral drone imagery to monitor crop health and recommend site-specific fertilizer applications

Crop Health Monitoring

Real-time assessment of crop condition using multispectral drone imagery and vegetation indices (NDVI, NDRE, GNDVI)

Computer Vision & ML

Developing scalable tools for agronomic models, yield prediction and Object detection

Geospatial Analysis

Advanced spatial analysis and modeling for agricultural planning and resource management
