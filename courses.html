Course Details & FAQs

What are the prerequisites?

Most beginner courses require no prior experience. Intermediate courses may require basic computer literacy. Advanced courses require relevant background knowledge.

Do you provide certificates?

Yes! All participants who complete the course receive a Agri-Intelligence Research Lab certificate of completion.

What's included in the course fee?

Course materials, hands-on instruction, software licenses (where applicable), meals, and certificate of completion.

Do you offer scholarships?

Yes, we offer scholarships for students and professionals from developing countries. Contact us for details.
