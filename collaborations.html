Building a network of partners advancing geospatial science

Our Partners

National Agriculture Research Council (NARC)

Academic & Research Partner

Primary partner for field research on precision rice management. NARC provides field sites, infrastructure, and research support for our studies in Khumaltar, Nepal.

Geospatial Community Organizations

Knowledge & Network Partner

Collaborating with open-source GIS communities and geospatial initiatives for knowledge sharing and capacity building.

International Research Institutions

Research & Innovation Partner

Working with universities and research centers globally to advance precision agriculture research and share best practices.

Technology & Agri-Tech Companies

Industry Partner

Partnerships with drone manufacturers and software companies to develop integrated solutions for precision agriculture.

Government Agencies

Policy & Development Partner

Collaborating with government institutions on climate-smart agriculture initiatives and sustainable development programs.

NGOs & Development Organizations

Impact & Implementation Partner

Working with development organizations to scale research findings and support farmer adoption of precision agriculture technologies.
